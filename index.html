Monthly Driver
Program

3 training days per month on weekends. Training at TGC Speedway or Dai Nam. Structured progression through Cadet, Junior, and Adult levels. Small groups with a maximum 6:1 driver to coach ratio.

  • Track sessions (1–2 hours per day)
  • Coaching: lines, braking, racecraft
  • On-board video review
  • Data analysis sessions
  • Simulator training (1 hour/day — remote with Blayke Philips)

Single Day
Training

For drivers of all levels — whether you're unsure about committing monthly, looking to fix bad habits, or pushing for final improvements. Sessions are tailored to the group's level on the day.
